Welcome to CTU

Safety and Security Facility

It is a facility intended for the safety and security of all university personnel, students, guests, and visitors. Buildings, grounds, and other campus assets are closely monitored, with a human workforce working 24/7 and installed with modern equipment like a turnstile RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) system at the entrance and CCTV cameras inside and outside campus premises.

New Administration Building (Ongoing Construction)

The most recent addition to the existing infrastructure of CTU Danao is the new administration building. It is the center of administration and management of the campus since the Campus Director, and other key officials are located in this three-story building. With this new administration officials' new dwelling place, a much better environment for all stakeholders in their transactions with CTU Danao is guaranteed.

Old Administration Building

This two-story building is the current location of the administration and other function offices. This has been a witness to the unwavering commitment of CTU Danao towards service, integrity, and excellence.

CTU Danao Hometel

Shake the hustle and bustle from the city and come to this 16-room unassuming oasis within the University where you can relax and enjoy a 360-degree view of the Northeastern Cebu landscape or take a dip in the newly-installed swimming pool and gaze over the eastern coastline by sunrise.

Student Activity Center (SAC)

The heart of the University's academic ceremonies and social and official functions are held in the SAC. This conventionally designed but fully-lighted and fully-air-conditioned activity center renders a dramatic transition between the contemporary CME, COE, and COED buildings to the accustomed CTU Danao configuration, and the linkage between the academic hub and the vibrant campus sports and cultural core.

COT (College of Technology) Building

This edifice is a convergence of the diverse industrial courses offered in CTU Danao. It houses the equipment, tools, and materials needed for an industrial technology student to meet the course's competencies. It allows the learner to holistically develop the "total being" while performing the technology-related competencies.

COE (College of Engineering) Building

A testament to CTU Danao’s commitment to advancing the institution’s premier brand, COE building provides a training ground for future champions, topnotch graduates, and engineers in five (5) engineering disciplines. Its classrooms and laboratory rooms are equipped with the necessary instruments and tools to deliver quality instruction and skills training to every engineering student.

COEd (College of Education) Building

The three-story building of the College of Education sits comfortably behind the newly-constructed administration building. The structure reflects modern-day classroom settings, equipped with Interactive White Boards, Smart Television sets, and furniture suited to students' needs. Each classroom's facilities would surely make learning more fun and exciting since the set-up permits ease in the group and individual activities, collaboration, and other student-centered activities. Furthermore, the Office of the College Dean and the office staff are on the second floor, while the faculty room is located on the third floor. The University provides well-maintained toilets on each floor.
